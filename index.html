Educational Scholarship Advisor

AI Expert System Project

The Educational Scholarship Advisor is a rule-based expert system designed to assist students in determining their eligibility for scholarships based on academic performance, family income, and extracurricular activities.

Use Case & System Design

Scenario: Lionel Messi, a student, enters his GPA, family income, and extracurricular details. The system evaluates his data against predefined IF-THEN rules to determine scholarship eligibility.

Key Stakeholders

  • Students: Seek guidance on scholarship eligibility.
  • Scholarship Organizations: Manage scholarship applications.
  • Parents: Provide financial details for evaluation.
  • Governments: Ensure fair and accessible funding distribution.

Decision Rules

The system follows a structured set of IF-THEN rules, such as:

  • IF GPA > 3.5 AND family income < $5,000 THEN eligible for scholarship.
  • IF GPA > 3.0 AND involved in extracurricular activities THEN eligible for partial scholarship.
  • IF GPA < 2.5 THEN ineligible; needs improvement in academics.
